Key Insights & Recommendations

Actual spends were 10% lower than planned, primarily due to drop in AOV & Sales.
Due to drop in volume for the month of December, prices were brought down in beginning of January to increase order volume and funnel. Result: ATC spiked by 6.5% and order volume increased by 25%.
As a retargeting bucket, we are running campaigns for BOF audience like website visitors, ATC and checkouts with dedicated budget of 10% of total budget to stay top of mind.
Testing 3-4 creatives per week to find at least 1 winning creative for performance stability. However, CTR for creatives hovered at 0.81% and no clear winner emerged.
To improve CTR, continuously discussing with client on influencer content based UGC/videos. This could help drive better CTR and results.
Aurora Mermaid Dress was a hero and best seller - most creatives featured it, but this didn't work out even with strong creative face (Niti Mohan).
Review-based creatives generally work for this category. Tried reviews and customer word creatives, but CTR was lower and didn't drive results.
Catalog has performed decently. Running it with different formats. Mid-Jan started collection ads which showed traction in the 1st week but then declined.
